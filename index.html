THE TABLE

A six-chamber revolver. A hooded figure across the felt. The cylinder is loaded with a blind mix of live rounds and blanks — you saw how many of each, once, before they disappeared into the gun.

Each turn: pick up the gun and choose. Aim it across the table, or press it to your own temple. A blank on yourself means you keep your turn. Anything else passes it.

Last one breathing wins.

WHAT'S ON THE TABLE

Between rounds, the table offers you things. Take them. You'll need every edge.

MAGNIFIER

See the current round in the chamber.

SHACKLES

The Dealer skips their next turn.

ELIXIR

Restore one life.

VENOM

Next live round deals double damage.

THE SWITCH

Flip the current round. Live becomes blank.

MOON CLIP

Eject the current round. You see what it was.

ROUNDS

Each set loads 2–6 rounds into the cylinder, always at least one live and one blank. You see the count once — then they vanish into the gun.

When the cylinder empties, a new set loads automatically and both players receive two items from the table. Adapt or die.
